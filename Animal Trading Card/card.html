Flying Squirell

Flying squirells cannot actually fly, instead the glide from tree to tree.

  • Scientific Name: Pteromyini
  • Average Length: 30 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 5 years
  • Habitat: North America and Asia

From cute and tiny to grow big as a house cat, flying squirrels can glide about 150ft and some can glow pink in the dark.
